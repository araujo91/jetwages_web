Privacy Policy – JetWages

Last updated: June 27, 2025

1. Local-First Data Approach

JetWages is designed to operate entirely offline whenever possible. All personal data — including schedules, payslips, pay details, and settings — is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage).

2. No Routine Data Transmission

JetWages does not send your data to external servers under normal usage. There is no cloud sync, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection.

3. File Processing & iOS Server Parsing

JetWages allows importing HTML and PDF files from eCrew and supplementary payslips. For most users and platforms, files are processed entirely on-device using expo-file-system and temporary memory operations.

Important for iOS Users: Due to technical limitations, PDF files imported on iOS devices are securely sent to our server for processing. The server extracts the relevant data and immediately returns it to your device. All uploaded files are permanently deleted from our server within minutes after processing. No personal information is stored beyond this temporary window.

4. Local Analytics (Optional)

JetWages tracks setup steps, file uploads, and historical pay calculations strictly for your personal use. These analytics:

  • Remain stored solely on your device
  • Do not contain sensitive personal details
  • Can be exported by you at any time via the Settings screen

5. Backup & Restore

You can export or import your JetWages data for backup purposes. These actions use your device's file system or sharing tools. No third-party services are involved in these operations.

6. Use of External Libraries

JetWages uses certain open-source libraries (e.g., for parsing PDF content) bundled within the app. These libraries operate fully offline and do not collect or transmit user data.

7. Your Data, Your Control

You maintain full control over your data. Uninstalling JetWages will permanently delete all app-related information from your device. JetWages never shares, sells, or transmits your data to third parties without your knowledge.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jetwages@gmail.com.
