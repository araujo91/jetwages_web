Privacy & Data Policy – JetWages

Last updated: June 28, 2025

1. Local-First Data Approach

JetWages is designed to operate entirely offline whenever possible. All personal data — including schedules, payslips, pay details, and settings — is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage).

2. No Routine Data Transmission

JetWages does not send your data to external servers under normal app usage. There is no cloud sync, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection.

3. File Processing & iOS Server Parsing

JetWages allows importing HTML schedules and PDF payslips from eCrew and supplementary sources. For most users and platforms, files are processed entirely on-device using expo-file-system and temporary memory operations.

Important for iOS Users: Due to technical limitations, PDF files imported on iOS devices are securely sent to our server for processing. The server extracts the relevant data and immediately returns it to your device. All uploaded files are permanently deleted from our server within minutes after processing. No personal information is stored beyond this temporary window.

4. Data Collection for Beta Access & Feedback

We may collect personal information such as your email address when you:

  • Sign up for beta testing
  • Provide feedback via forms or surveys
  • Contact us directly

This data is used exclusively to communicate app updates, collect feedback, and improve JetWages. Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties.

5. Feedback & User Submissions

Any feedback you provide may be stored for improvement purposes. You can request the deletion of your feedback and associated personal information at any time by contacting us.

6. Local Analytics (Optional)

JetWages tracks setup steps, file uploads, and pay calculations strictly for your personal use. These analytics:

  • Remain stored solely on your device
  • Do not contain sensitive personal details
  • Can be exported by you at any time via the Settings screen

7. Backup & Restore

You can export or import your JetWages data for backup purposes. These actions use your device's file system or sharing tools. No third-party services are involved in these operations.

8. Use of External Libraries

JetWages uses certain open-source libraries (e.g., for parsing PDF content) bundled within the app. These libraries operate fully offline and do not collect or transmit user data.

9. Your Data, Your Control

You maintain full control over your data. Uninstalling JetWages will permanently delete all app-related information from your device. You can request deletion of any email or feedback data by contacting us directly.

10. Contact

For questions, data requests, or concerns about this Privacy & Data Policy, please contact us at jetwages@gmail.com.
