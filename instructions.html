üöÄ Using JetWages

Navigation Menu

Tap the top-right menu to access key areas such as Upload Calendar, Distance Calculator, Sales Comparison, and Settings.

Home Calendar

The calendar shows your duties for the month. Tap any day to view or edit flights, sales, and extras for that date.

Edit Flights & Duty

Add, remove or change flights for the selected day. You can also add custom duties using the "Add Duty" option.

Add Extras

Input extra payments for the day, such as delay payments, DDO/WFDO, or Night Stop allowances.

Add Sales

Record your onboard sales for each day. The app will automatically calculate commission based on your input.

Distance Calculator

Quickly calculate the distance between two airports. Enter airport names or IATA codes to get the result.

Sales Comparison

Upload your official supplementary sales PDF to compare your reported commission with the app's calculation.

Review Differences

See a side-by-side comparison for each day. Positive or negative differences highlight potential discrepancies.
